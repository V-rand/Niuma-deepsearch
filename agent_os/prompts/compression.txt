First, analyze the conversation in <analysis> tags. This is a drafting scratchpad — work through each message chronologically to organize your thoughts before writing the final summary.

In your analysis:
1. Chronologically walk through each message and section of the conversation. For each, identify:
   - The user's explicit requests and intents
   - Your approach and tool calls
   - Key decisions, concepts, and findings
   - Specific details: file names, paths, data points, full tool output where relevant
   - Errors and how you fixed them — pay special attention to user feedback that told you to change your approach
2. Double-check for accuracy and completeness before closing the analysis.
